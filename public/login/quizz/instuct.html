MCQ Test Instructions

Prepare, Perform, Progress

General Guidelines

  • The test consists of 15 questions divided equally among three subjects: Math, Physics, and Chemistry (5 questions each).
  • The test duration is 15 minutes.
  • Time recommendations:
    • Math: 5 minutes
    • Physics: 5 minutes
    • Chemistry: 5 minutes
  • The test will automatically close when the timer ends.
  • Manage your time wisely to complete all questions!

How to Attempt

  1. Read the question carefully.
  2. Select one of the four options provided for each question.
  3. Click "Next" to move to the next question.
  4. You can navigate back to previous questions if needed.

Progress Feedback

After completing the test, you will receive:

  • Your total score
  • Subject-wise performance analysis
  • Suggestions for improvement
